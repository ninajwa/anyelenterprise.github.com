Recipe


Popia Chicken Spaghetti

  1. Boil spaghetti until cooked

  2. Satiate the garlic, then add the contents of the chicken

  3. Add salt and sugar

  4. Then insert the boiled spaghetti into the chicken coating

  5. Then, serve for popia with insert the filling into the popia skin

  6. Prepare for the popia skin

  7. Fill the popia with the popia filling that has been prepared into popia skin

  8. Wrap the popia skin and the filling by wiping eggs

  9. Then, serve for the popia sauce which is Bolognaise sauce

  10. Saute garlic and put the chicken in and then add the tomato flavored sauce

  11. Add salt and sugar to taste

  12. Spaghetti Popia already can be serve with bolognaise sauce
